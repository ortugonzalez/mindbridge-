# BRESO — Soledad 🌱
> "A todos nos hubiese gustado que nos ayudaran a entender lo que pasaba a nuestro alrededor."

## The Problem
In Latin America, 80% of people with mental health issues never access treatment. The barriers are cost, stigma, and lack of accessible support systems.

## The Solution
BRESO is an AI mental wellness agent powered by Soledad, an AI companion that:
- Has daily check-in conversations with users
- Detects emotional patterns and crisis signals
- Alerts trusted family contacts when needed
- Coordinates professional support
- Processes subscriptions autonomously via x402 on Celo

## How It Works
1. User signs up and chats daily with Soledad
2. Soledad builds memory and detects patterns over time
3. If crisis signals detected → family contact notified immediately
4. Subscription payments processed in USDT on Celo via x402
5. DeFi cashback: 30% of yield generated by subscription funds returned monthly

## Crisis Detection
Three-level alert system:
- 🟡 Yellow: 24h no check-in → gentle family email
- 🟠 Orange: 48h no check-in or 3 negative days → urgent alert
- 🔴 Red: Crisis keywords detected → immediate push notification
